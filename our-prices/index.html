{How many does it feed?}

 

 

 

Single Tier Cakes
6″ round: 10-12

Multi-Tiered Cakes:
5″ + 6″ round: 16-18

Two Layer Cakes:
13×9: 40-48

Our servings are calculated using a 1x2x5 serving size.  Our tiered cakes are 3 layers of delicious cake and stand 5″-6″ tall. This allows you to feed more guests with cake slices being larger than standard-sized cakes. Cake servings can vary depending on how you cut. To get the most servings, we always recommend cutting rectangles rather than pie slices. Cake cutting guides are available upon request.

All cakes are frosted with fresh-made buttercream. Fondant cakes have a layer of tasty, fresh-made fondant around the cake and buttercream.

Retainers:

All orders $35 or less require full payment at time of booking.
Orders over $35 require a 50% retainer to be paid at the time of booking.
This retainer is non-refundable and is applied to your final balance at pickup.
All decorating parties require $50 retainer at booking and remaining balance paid no later than 3 days prior to party.

Delivery Charges:

All other delivery dates may include a minimum fee of $10 + $.55/mile.

*All prices subject to change.*
